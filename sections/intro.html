Welcome to the Wild Atlantic Way

Explore the rugged beauty and rich culture of Ireland’s spectacular west coast

The Wild Atlantic Way stretches across 2,500 km of scenic coastline — from Donegal to Cork — offering unique landscapes, authentic experiences, and unforgettable moments.

Where is the Wild Atlantic Way?

The route hugs the western coast of Ireland, from the northern tips of Donegal to the southern edges of Cork. It's a journey through charming towns, stunning cliffs, and warm welcomes.
